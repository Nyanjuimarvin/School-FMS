WHAT WE DO

We provide a secure,efficient and paperless way for school accountants to keep records of the school's investsments and inventory.
and for bursars to keep track of fees balances and students pocket money.

OUR VISION

To make our application accessible to all schools in Kenya.

OUR STORY

We came up with the application due to the tension between the administration and parents in a majority of schools where the latter feel they pay too much for the services the students receive.
We also wanted to reduce theft in schools by having the bursar keep the students' money and hand them when needed.
